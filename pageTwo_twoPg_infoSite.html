Classic Favorites

  • Chanel Bleu De Chanel Eau De Parfum

    Notes: Fresh and woody scent with citrus, sandalwood, and cedar notes.
    Occasion: Suitable for both casual and formal settings.
    Longevity: Long-lasting, typically 8+ hours on the skin.
    History: Launched in 2010, Bleu De Chanel was created by perfumer Jacques Polge
    to embody the essence of freedom and elegance with a modern twist.

  • Paco Rabanne Invictus

    Notes: Fresh and sporty scent with grapefruit, marine notes, and ambergris.
    Occasion: Perfect for daytime wear and active lifestyles.
    Longevity: Moderate to long-lasting, typically 6-8 hours on the skin.
    History: Introduced in 2013, Invictus by Paco Rabanne was designed by perfumers
    Anne Flipo and Christophe Raynaud to capture the spirit of victory and strength.

  • Tom Ford Noir Extreme

    Notes: Warm and oriental scent with cardamom, amber, and vanilla notes.
    Occasion: Ideal for evening wear and special occasions.
    Longevity: Long-lasting, typically 8+ hours on the skin.
    History: Launched in 2015, Noir Extreme by Tom Ford was created to evoke
    a sense of mystery and sophistication with its rich and complex composition.

  • Versace Eros Eau De Parfum

    Notes: Fresh and oriental scent with mint, tonka bean, and vanilla notes.
    Occasion: Suitable for both day and night wear.
    Longevity: Long-lasting, typically 8+ hours on the skin.
    History: Introduced in 2012, Eros by Versace was crafted by perfumers
    Aurelien Guichard and Olivier Pescheux to embody passion and desire.
